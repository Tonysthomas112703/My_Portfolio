Recent Projects

Project 1

Driver Drowsiness Detection System

This project utilizes computer vision techniques to monitor the driver's facial features and detect signs of drowsiness in real-time. By analyzing facial expressions and eye movements, the system alerts the driver with visual and auditory signals if signs of drowsiness are detected, enhancing road safety. The integration of Raspberry Pi ensures a compact and efficient solution for real-world implementation.

Project 2

Smart Walking Stick for Blind

A smart walking stick for the blind with computer vision uses cameras and image processing to detect obstacles. Equipped with a camera, it captures the environment, and the system provides real-time feedback through vibrations or audible alerts when obstacles are detected. This enhances the mobility and safety of visually impaired individuals, allowing them to navigate their surroundings more confidently.
